About Me

From digital textile research to software development

My journey into software development began with digital textile research during my undergraduate studies. Working with a startup, I discovered the power of web development and script automation to solve real-world problems in my field.

This experience sparked my curiosity about programming and led me to pursue a comprehensive Full Stack curriculum. I found myself drawn to the intersection of creativity and technology, where I could build solutions that make a meaningful impact.

Today, I specialize in creating robust web applications using modern technologies like Typescript, React, GraphQL, Design Systems, REST, and more. I'm passionate about writing clean, maintainable code and creating user experiences that are both beautiful and functional.
